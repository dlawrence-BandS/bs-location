Flight detail

Click a flight in the scoreboard or the list — the analysis runs automatically. "Media regions" are where the plan bought media; "comparison regions" got none (and have no B&S stores), acting as the what-would-have-happened-anyway benchmark. Both are indexed to their own pre-flight average (100) — if the media line pulls away from the comparison during and after the flight, the campaign moved the needle. Weather, promos and distribution differences between regions aren't controlled for; promo windows and the 30 Jun 2026 delivery change are marked on the chart.
